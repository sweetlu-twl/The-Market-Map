🛡️ Level 4: Risk & Survival

This is where the game really begins. Every trader has winning trades. Only the disciplined ones survive the drawdowns.

🎯 Objective

Learn how to stay in the game by controlling your risk and protecting your capital.

  • Understand how to size positions relative to account size and risk tolerance
  • Learn about stop losses, risk/reward ratios, and capital preservation
  • Develop emotional habits for surviving slumps, drawdowns, and overconfidence

🔍 Terms to Know

  • Risk per Trade: The dollar amount you're willing to lose on a single trade
  • Position Size: The number of shares/contracts based on your risk level
  • Drawdown: The decline from a peak account value to a low
  • Risk/Reward: The ratio of potential gain vs potential loss
  • Stop Loss: A predetermined price to cut the trade if it goes against you
  • Max Daily Loss: A rule that tells you when to stop trading for the day

Track all of this inside the Disciplined Trader Journal.

📚 Wisdom from the Greats

  • “The most important thing is to play great defense.” — Paul Tudor Jones
  • “In the long run, you’re trading your beliefs, not the market.” — Mark Douglas
  • “I always sell down to the sleeping point.” — Jesse Livermore

Risk management isn’t about being cautious — it’s about staying alive long enough to get lucky.

🧠 Why This Matters

  • Most traders blow up not from one trade — but from not knowing when to stop.
  • Capital is your freedom. If you lose it, the game is over. If you protect it, you're always in the game.
  • Confidence comes from survival. Every risk rule you set is a vote for your future self.

🎮 Your Mission

Build a personal risk profile and test it live.

  • ✅ Set your max risk per trade (start with 1% or less)
  • ✅ Use the calculator in the journal to size a position
  • ✅ Define a stop loss and risk/reward before entering
  • ✅ If hit your max daily loss — stop. No revenge trades.
  • ✅ Log your emotions when losing — do you spiral or stay calm?

Survival isn’t optional. It’s the whole game. Master this level and you’ll outlast 90% of traders.
